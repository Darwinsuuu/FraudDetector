FraudDetector

Protecting You from Fraud, One Detection at a Time

FraudDetector is a tool designed to analyze email content and identify potential signs of fraudulent activity. Using advanced algorithms and pattern recognition, it helps users detect phishing attempts, scams, or suspicious messages, ensuring a safer digital communication experience.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

FraudDetector utilizes machine learning algorithms and natural language processing to analyze email content for red flags, such as suspicious language patterns, phishing links, or known scam indicators. It cross-references these elements with a database of fraudulent activities to provide an accurate assessment of potential risks.

Machine learning algorithms improve fraud detection by analyzing large datasets to identify hidden patterns and trends that may not be immediately obvious. As the system learns from previous data, it becomes better at detecting subtle signs of fraud, adapting to new fraud tactics over time. This makes it more accurate and efficient in identifying potential fraudulent activities.

If FraudDetector flags an email as fraudulent, avoid responding to it or clicking on any links. Mark the email as spam or phishing in your email client and delete it. Additionally, consider reporting the email to your email provider or a relevant authority to help prevent further scams.

About Us

Welcome to the Fraud Email Detector app!

The Fraud Email Detector app is our capstone project at the University of the Cordilleras, Philippines. Designed to help users detect and avoid email scams, this app serves as a practical tool for online safety and fraud prevention.

We are proud to share this project and hope it provides value to its users. Thank you for supporting our work!
